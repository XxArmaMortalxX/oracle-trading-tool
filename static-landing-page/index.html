HOW IT WORKS

The Algorithm Pipeline

Four stages. Fully automated. Delivered before the opening bell.

PHASE 1

INGEST

Scans Reddit, X, and TikTok for stock ticker mentions. Extracts post text, engagement metrics, and timestamps from 3 subreddits, cashtag searches, and trending videos.

r/wallstreetbets r/pennystocks $cashtags TikTok Search
PHASE 2

ANALYZE

NLP sentiment classifier processes every mention. LLM-based primary tier handles sarcasm, negation, and context. Enhanced keyword fallback ensures 100% coverage.

Bullish Detection Bearish Detection Negation Handling
PHASE 3

DETECT

Compares current sentiment snapshots against historical baselines. Flags dramatic shifts (bearish → bullish) with severity classification: DRAMATIC, MODERATE, or MINOR.

Shift Alerts Velocity Tracking Momentum Scoring
PHASE 4

DELIVER

Locks the top 20 stocks at market open. Calculates entry signals, support/resistance levels, and risk-reward ratios. Delivers scored picks to your dashboard with bias indicators.
